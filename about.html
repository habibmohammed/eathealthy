A little bit about Us

Background Story

Our company started its journey in 2008 when our CEO Kevin Boris took a trip to farmers market. The experience he had lead him to realize that shopping organic food is not an easy thing to do let alone preparing it the right way. So he founded this company as means to deliver organic foods only, but since then has grown to include more services.

Eat Healthy now has organic farms throught US to provide fresh fruits and vegitables on time. We also have a program that benefits our small local farmers by distributing their products.
